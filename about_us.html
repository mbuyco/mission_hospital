About Us

What is Mission Hospital?

Mission Hospital is a health service cooperative under Medical Mission Group Metro Manila East Hospital and Health Services Cooperative that provides hospital services with high quality healthcare delivery system to all communities. We offer competent medical team thru highly specialized doctors, nurses, pharmacists, medical technologists, radiology technicians and accounting staff.

Vision

To provide quality health care that is acceptable, accessible, adequate and affordable to all.

Mission

To be regarded as a competitive cooperative hospital responsive to the health needs of the community.

Core Values

A committed health service cooperative institution composed of God-loving members dedicated in the service of sustaining life with utmost quality, honesty and transparency.

Goal

The MMG MME HHSC shall embody the ideals of a genuine Cooperative by extending systematic, transparent and efficient services to all its stakeholders and to the community, especially those who are in need of basic healthcare.

Mission Hospital located at Km 17 Ortigas Avenue Extension, Brgy. Rosario, Pasig City is a Level I hospital duly accredited by the Department of Health and PhilHealth.

The hospital building is a 5 storey structure with a 40 bed capacity, composed of 7 air-conditioned private rooms, 16 beds in semi-private rooms and 14 beds in the air-conditioned wards. The building also houses the Operating, Delivery, Recovery, Nursery and Emergency Rooms and has an in-house pharmacy and chapel.

The hospital offers the following medical specialties: Surgery, Anesthesiology, Obstetrics & Gynecology, Pediatrics, Ophthalmology, Internal Medicine, Orthopedics, Dentistry, Radiology and Pathology. It also has a separate Physical Therapy, Respiratory and Hemodialysis Unit.

The hospital currently services the needs of the various communities and companies around it.

Mission Hospital Pasig is owned and operated by the Medical Mission Group-Metro Manila East-Hospital and Health Services Cooperative (MMG-MME-HHSC), a group of physicians and citizens who have banded together to form a cooperative specializing in health services. Officially registered with the Cooperative Development Authority (CDA), it is the fastest growing cooperative hospital in the country today.

Mission Hospital Pasig has just launched its newest facility, the Mission Medical Plaza (MMP) Building, a 5 storey building just a few meters away from the main Mission Hospital building.

The MMP houses the medical clinics, Computed Tomography (CT) Scan, Hemodialysis, Merci Cardiovascular Clinic, a cafeteria and spa.

Currently, Mission Hospital Pasig is managed by its CEO/Medical Director Dr. Roel Tito A. Marcial and Hospital Administrator Dr. Maria Donna Ancilla C. Vega, while Dr. Eugenio Alonzo is the Chief of Clinics.
